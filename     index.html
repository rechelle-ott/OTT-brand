SWOT

What the brand is strong at — and what can quietly kill growth.

The honest read: OTT has strong cultural equity and event quality, but the growth ceiling is capped until membership, owned media, reporting, and repeat touchpoints are built.

✅ Strengths

7 years of authentic community trust.
Premium event identity and strong cultural specificity.
The Blend is a repeatable flagship IP with monthly potential.
Strong partner roster and sponsorship credibility.
Travel and luxury social events differentiate OTT from local groups.

⚠️ Weaknesses

11K followers after 7 years means visibility is under-indexed.
Event content is not being repurposed into a media engine.
Membership benefits and tiers are not clearly packaged.
Wellness and pro development lack year-round cadence.
Too much reliance on Instagram and event-by-event promotion.

⬆️ Opportunities

Build The Blend into a multi-city/event media property.
Launch paid membership with tiered access and lounge value.
Use travel content as the premium aspiration driver.
Turn Brunch & Build into corporate/ERG and LinkedIn-friendly programming.
Build sponsor reporting that competitors cannot match.

▼ Threats

Toasted Life has stronger media/geographic footprint.
Town Strides wins on frequency, accessibility, and wellness momentum.
Tribe Collective overlaps on membership/travel/community.
FRNDSOnly overlaps on friendship/social discovery.
If OTT does not productize membership, another brand will own that lane.
